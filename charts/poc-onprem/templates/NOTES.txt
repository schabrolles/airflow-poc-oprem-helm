================================================================================
⚠️  IMPORTANT NOTES
================================================================================

1. LLM Services Startup Time:
   - Ollama services may take 5-10 minutes to download and load models
   - Monitor with: kubectl logs -f deployment/ollama-llm-embedding -n {{ include "poc-onprem.namespace" . }}

2. Airflow Logs PVC:
   - Requires ReadWriteMany (RWX) storage class
   - Ensure your cluster supports RWX (NFS, CephFS, GlusterFS, etc.)

3. Database Initialization:
   - PostgreSQL databases will initialize on first start
   - Wait for databases to be ready before other services start

4. Security:
   - Change all default passwords immediately in production
   - Consider using external secret management (Sealed Secrets, Vault, etc.)
   - Review and adjust resource limits based on your workload

5. Image Registry:
   - Update image registry in values.yaml: global.imageRegistry
   - Ensure all images are built and pushed to your registry
